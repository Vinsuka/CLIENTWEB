Our Services

Topcon specialises in integrated Telecom Infrastructure Development and Air-Conditioning & Refrigeration solutions — supply, installation and maintenance, plus safe site decommissioning.

Telecom Infrastructure Development

  • Supply & installation of equipment cabins / shelters
  • Internal/external cable ladders & earthing systems
  • Alarm & safety systems (AC fault, power/phase failure, heat/smoke detectors)
  • Tower & monopole fabrication and erection; antenna brackets
  • Site decommissioning & safe dismantling

Air-Conditioning & Refrigeration

  • Industrial & domestic AC systems
  • Cold rooms and refrigeration systems
  • AC systems for telecom base stations with automated control logic
  • Repairs, maintenance, fabrication of AC control panels

About Us

Company Overview

Topcon Air-conditioning Company started in 2002 and is an integrated services org. specialising in supply, installation and maintenance of telecom equipment cabins, pre-fabricated housing, industrial & domestic AC and refrigeration, cold rooms, and electrical installations.

Mission

Provide the customer with professional and excellent service in telecom infrastructure developments, air-conditioning & refrigeration and allied services.

Vision

To be the leader in Telecom Infrastructure Development of the industry.
